It won't cost a leg & a dollar

A RESPITE FOR THE DISCERNING PROFESSIONAL

Located just a hop, skip, and a jump away from your desk, Bar Terry is strategically placed to help you escape from the chaos of your 9-to-5. Bar Terry is not your typical bar; it's your escape hatch from the mundane. Our swanky yet approachable atmosphere is the ideal place to unwind with coworkers who understand the pain of endless meetings and that one coworker who always microwaves fish.

We've got classics, we've got signature concoctions, and we've even got one named after that client who just can't decide what they want - "The Indecision Elixir." It's the drink equivalent of "I'll get back to you on that."

Our commitment to affordability means you can enjoy the finer things in life without a hefty tab. After all, it won't cost you a leg and a dollar.
